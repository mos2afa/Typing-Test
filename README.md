-------------------------*Typing Modes*---------------------------

--- Time mode: 15s, 30s, 60s, 120s.

--- Word mode: 10, 25, 50, 100 words.

--- Live WPM updated every second.

-------------------------*Customization*--------------------------

--- Full color customization for the entire app.

--- All preferences are saved to a JSON file.

--- Ability to import or export settings as JSON.

--- Option to reset all settings to default.

-----------------------*Progress Tracking*------------------------

--- Results stored locally in a SQLite database.

--- Export results as an Excel file.

--- Button to clear all saved results.

--- New high score indicator:
		Displays a star icon.
		Shows the difference between the
		previous best WPM and the new score.

-----------------------*Language Support*-------------------------

--- Supports 15 of the most popular languages worldwide.

-----------------------*User Experience*--------------------------

--- Full-screen mode.

--- Responsive, accurate, and smooth typing test behavior.
